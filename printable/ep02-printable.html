Week 2: Animals — Printable Game Materials

5 days · 5 missions · Save the zoo animals!

📋 Parent Setup Guide (10 minutes)

Read this before you print!

🔦 Day 1 — Zoo Animal Hunt (Card Hunt)

Print pages 2-3 double-sided (flip on short edge). Cut out the 4 animal cards.

Hide them BEFORE your child starts the game:

🐵 Monkey card → somewhere high up (like on a shelf or top of a cabinet)

🦁 Lion card → under a blanket or cushion (the king's "den")

🐼 Panda card → on something soft and cozy (bed, sofa, or plush toy)

🐯 Tiger card → behind a door or curtain (tigers love to ambush!)

The cards spell W-I-L-D when all 4 are found. The letter is on the back!

🌈 Day 2 — Animal Colors

No printing needed! Your child will mix colors by dragging on screen, then find real objects matching: pink, light blue, and brown. Have some colorful items around (pink clothes, blue cups, brown toys, etc.).

🎵 Day 3 — Farm Sounds

No printing needed! The game plays farm animal sounds: cow, sheep, horse, pig, and chicken. Optional fun: make the animal sounds yourself from another room! Can your child tell which animal is mooing?

🤸 Day 4 — Animal Dance

No printing needed! Make sure there's open floor space. Your child will hop like a rabbit, stomp like an elephant, swim like a fish, fly like a bird, and crawl like a crab. Join in! Kids love it when parents act silly too!

❤️ Day 5 — Animal Rescue (YOU are the hero!)

Important preparation:

1. Print the Heart Piece card (page 4) and keep it with you.

2. Prepare a small toy animal or draw a cute animal together on paper.

3. When the game says "Find your Earth Helper," give your child the Heart Piece card. The code is 738.

4. Later, present the toy/drawn animal — this is the "rescued baby animal" that found its mommy!

This is about empathy and caring. Praise your child for being kind to animals!
