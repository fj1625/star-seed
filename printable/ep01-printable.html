🌟 The Star Seed

Episode 1: My Home — Printable Game Materials

5 days · 5 missions · 1 magical star seed

📋 Parent Setup Guide (10 minutes)

Read this before you print!

🔦 Day 1 — Hidden Star Fragments (Card Hunt)

Print pages 2-3 double-sided (flip on short edge). Cut out the 4 cards.

Hide them BEFORE your child starts the game:

☀️ Sun card → near a window (where light comes in)

🌳 Tree card → near a wooden chair or table

🍎 Apple card → in or near the refrigerator

🐰 Rabbit card → on the sofa, bed, or a soft pillow

Make it fun! Don't hide them too well — the magic is in finding, not searching forever.

🌈 Day 2 — Color Detective

No printing needed! Your child will find real objects around the home that match the target colors. Make sure there are some colorful items accessible (red fruit, blue cup, yellow toys, etc.).

🎵 Day 3 — Sound Password

No printing needed! The game uses voice synthesis for animal sounds. Optional fun: you can make the animal sounds yourself from another room to surprise your child!

🤸 Day 4 — Star Dance

No printing needed! Just make sure there's some open floor space for your child to move around safely. Maybe join in — it's more fun!

❤️ Day 5 — Heart of the Star (YOU are the star!)

Important preparation:

1. Print the Heart Piece card (page 4) and keep it in your pocket.

2. Prepare a real seed + a small pot with soil (bean seeds or sunflower seeds work great — they sprout fast!).

3. When the game says "Find your Earth Helper," your child will come find YOU. Give them the Heart Piece card. Then later, give them the seed to plant!

This is the big finale — the seed becomes "Twinkle" in the real world. Water it together every day!
